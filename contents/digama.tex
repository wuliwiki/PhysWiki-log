\pentry{Gamma函数\upref{Gamma}}

\footnote{参考 Wikipedia \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digamma_function}{相关页面}。}定义\textbf{双 Gamma 函数（digamma function）}为
\begin{equation}
\psi(x) = \ln[\Gamma(x)]' = \frac{\Gamma'(x)}{\Gamma(x)}
\end{equation}
